# Permanente Health Plan Digital Member Portal User Manual

## Purpose

This manual explains how members can use the Permanente Health Plan Digital Member Portal to sign in with credentials provided by the administrator or staff, manage their profile, request appointments, communicate with staff, view prescriptions, and track updates.

## What You Can Do in the Portal

The member portal lets you:

- sign in using your email address or Member ID
- review health plan information and covered medicines
- complete and update your member profile
- request and monitor appointments
- pay for appointments when payment is required
- receive real-time appointment and message alerts
- chat with the administration team
- view prescriptions from completed appointments

## Before You Start

Prepare the following before using the portal:

- a stable internet connection
- the login credentials given to you by the administrator or staff
- your Member ID or email address, if included with your issued credentials
- your password

Important:

- Members do not create accounts by themselves in this workflow.
- Your administrator or staff gives you the credentials needed to access the portal.
- Keep your Member ID and password in a safe place because you can use them to sign in later.
- Some features, especially appointment booking, work best after your profile is fully completed.

## Accessing the Portal

1. Open the Permanente Health Plan Digital Member Portal in your browser.
2. On the landing page, open the member login page.
3. Enter the credentials provided by the administrator or staff.
4. Continue to the authenticated member area after a successful sign-in.

## Getting Your Login Credentials

Before your first login, you must receive your portal credentials from the administrator or staff.

These credentials may include:

- your Member ID
- your email address
- your password

If you do not yet have working credentials:

1. Contact the clinic administrator or staff.
2. Ask for your member portal login details.
3. Verify whether you should sign in with your Member ID or email address.
4. Keep the credentials private after receiving them.

## Signing In

You can sign in using either:

- your email address and password
- your Member ID and password

Steps:

1. Open the login page.
2. Enter your email address or Member ID.
3. Enter your password.
4. If you want the portal to remember your login details on the device, enable Remember Me.
5. Select Log In.

After a successful login:

- the portal verifies your member account
- the system confirms that your account is active
- you are taken to the authenticated member area

Important:

- If your account has been deactivated, you will not be allowed to sign in.
- If your account exists but your member profile is missing, contact support.
- If your issued credentials do not work, ask the administrator or staff to verify them.

## Main Navigation

After signing in, the main member sidebar gives access to these sections:

- Information
- Profile
- Appointment
- Chat

Additional portal features include:

- real-time popup notifications
- notification history through the notification bell
- prescription viewing for completed appointments
- payment status updates during appointment processing

## Information Section

Use the Information section to review general member resources.

In this section you can:

- search covered medicines
- open medicine details and dosage information
- review available services
- check diagnostic laboratory package information
- read health education content
- view schedule and clinic hours
- find contact details for the health plan

How to use it:

1. Select Information from the sidebar.
2. Use the medicine search bar to find a medicine name or keyword.
3. Select a medicine or service card to open more details.
4. Scroll through the page to review schedules, services, and contact information.

## Completing and Updating Your Profile

The Profile section is one of the most important parts of the portal. You should complete it before requesting an appointment.

### Basic Profile Information

Your profile includes:

- first name
- middle name
- last name
- suffix
- Member ID
- age
- gender
- email
- phone number
- home address
- birthday
- profile image

### Medical Information

You can also maintain medical details such as:

- blood type
- allergies
- current medications
- medical conditions
- surgical history
- family history
- blood transfusion history
- other medical notes

### How to Update Your Profile

1. Select Profile from the sidebar.
2. Review your current information.
3. Switch to edit mode if needed.
4. Update your personal and medical details.
5. Save the changes.

Recommendation:

- Complete at least your name, age, gender, phone number, and address before trying to book an appointment.

### Changing Your Password

If the profile page displays the password section, you can:

1. Enter your current password.
2. Enter a new password.
3. Confirm the new password.
4. Save the password change.

## Booking an Appointment

The Appointment section is used to request new appointments and monitor existing ones.

### Available Services

The portal supports appointment requests for these service categories:

- Imaging
- Laboratory
- Doctor's Consultation

Examples of sub-services include:

- X-ray
- ECG
- Ultrasound
- CBC
- FBS
- Cholesterol
- Lipid Profile
- Travel Clearance
- New Prescription
- Prescription Refills
- Routine Wellness Check Up
- Imaging Request
- Laboratory Request

### Before Booking

Make sure that:

- you are logged in
- your profile is complete
- you choose a valid working day
- you select an available time slot

### How to Request an Appointment

1. Select Appointment from the sidebar.
2. Choose your preferred date.
3. Select an available time slot.
4. Choose a service category.
5. Select one or more related sub-services if required.
6. Enter the reason for your visit if prompted.
7. Submit the appointment request.

After submission:

- your request is saved as pending
- the system shows a confirmation message
- a confirmation message is also posted to your chat thread
- payment status is initially tracked as unpaid when payment is required

### Important Appointment Rules

- You may be blocked from booking if your profile is incomplete.
- The system does not allow appointments on blocked non-working days.
- If a slot is already taken, it will not appear as available.
- In some cases, duplicate appointment requests for the same date may be prevented.

## Understanding Appointment Statuses

Your appointment may show one of several statuses. Common meanings are below.

- Pending: Your request was submitted and is waiting for review.
- Accepted or Confirmed: Your appointment has been approved.
- Scheduled: Your appointment has been set in the system.
- Rescheduled: Your appointment date or time was changed and approved.
- Declined: Your request was not approved.
- Completed: Your visit was finished.
- Missed: The appointment date passed and the visit was not completed.
- Cancellation Requested or Pending: A cancellation request is under review.
- Cancelled: The appointment was cancelled.

## Managing Existing Appointments

The Appointment page separates records into upcoming and past appointments.

### Upcoming Appointments

Use this area to:

- review confirmed or pending visits
- check appointment date and time
- monitor approval status
- follow payment status
- receive reminders and updates

### Past Appointments

Use this area to:

- review completed visits
- open appointment details
- review notes or remarks when available

### Updating the Appointment Reason

If the portal allows editing the reason for your appointment:

1. Open the selected appointment.
2. Choose the option to edit the reason.
3. Enter the updated reason.
4. Save the change.

## Payment Flow

Some appointments may require payment processing.

### If Payment Succeeds

- the portal displays a payment success message
- the appointment payment status is marked as paid
- you are redirected back to the Appointment page

### If Payment Is Cancelled

- the portal displays a payment cancelled message
- no successful payment is recorded
- you are redirected back to the Appointment page so you can try again later

## Notifications and Alerts

The portal includes real-time notifications for member updates.

You may receive alerts for:

- new admin chat messages
- appointment acceptance
- appointment decline
- reschedule approval
- completion updates
- cancellation approval or decline
- refund updates when applicable

How notifications appear:

- popup toasts appear on the screen for new updates
- a notification bell shows unread counts
- notification history is kept so you can review recent alerts

Best practice:

- check notifications regularly after requesting an appointment
- open your chat if a notification mentions appointment details or staff guidance

## Using Chat

The Chat section is your direct communication channel with the administration team.

You can use it to:

- receive appointment-related messages
- ask follow-up questions
- view system-generated appointment confirmations
- stay informed about updates from staff

### How to Use Chat

1. Select Chat from the sidebar.
2. Wait for the conversation to load.
3. Read the latest messages.
4. Type your message in the input box.
5. Send the message.

Chat tips:

- appointment confirmations may appear automatically in chat after booking
- unread messages can trigger notifications
- opening chat helps you review the full conversation history

## Viewing Prescriptions

Prescription records are available for completed appointments.

What you can do:

- review medicines linked to a completed visit
- see the appointment date associated with the prescription
- generate or download a PDF prescription copy when available

### How to View Prescriptions

1. Open the prescription area if it is available in your member flow.
2. Wait for the system to load prescriptions linked to your completed appointments.
3. Select the prescription you want to review.
4. Generate the PDF if you need a printable copy.

Important:

- If you do not yet have any completed appointments, no prescriptions will appear.

## Logging Out

When you are finished using the portal:

1. Select the Logout button.
2. Confirm the logout action when prompted.
3. Wait for the portal to return to the public landing page.

Recommendation:

- Always log out when using a shared device.

## Troubleshooting

### I cannot log in

Check the following:

- you entered the correct email or Member ID
- you entered the correct password
- your account is active
- your internet connection is stable

### I forgot my Member ID

Try these options:

- check the login details previously given to you by the administrator or staff
- review any onboarding record provided by the clinic or administrator
- contact support for account verification

### I cannot book an appointment

Possible reasons:

- your profile is incomplete
- the selected day is not available for booking
- the time slot is already taken
- you already have a conflicting appointment request

### I am not receiving notifications

Check whether:

- you are still signed in
- your browser allows notifications and local storage
- you opened the portal long enough for live updates to load

### My payment did not finish

If payment is interrupted or cancelled:

- return to the Appointment page
- verify whether the appointment still shows unpaid
- try the payment again if instructed by staff or the portal

## Quick Start Summary

If you want the shortest path to using the portal successfully, follow this order:

1. Get your login credentials from the administrator or staff.
2. Log in using your Member ID or email and password.
3. Complete your profile.
4. Open Information to review services and covered medicines.
5. Request an appointment.
6. Watch for notifications and chat updates.
7. Review prescriptions after completed visits.
8. Log out when finished.
